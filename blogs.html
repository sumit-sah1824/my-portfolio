Gradient Descent Intuition

Consider the quadratic loss function: \\( f(x) = \\frac{1}{2}x^2 \\)

Its gradient is \\( \\nabla f(x) = x \\), and the update rule becomes:

\\[ x_{t+1} = x_t - \\eta x_t = (1 - \\eta)x_t \\]

We see exponential decay when \\( 0 < \\eta < 1 \\).
